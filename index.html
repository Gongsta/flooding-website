Forecasting Floods using Big Data and AI.


Floods are the most common and devastating type of natural disasters in our century. A recent report showed that in the last decade, over 2.3 billion people have affected by flooding events.

What is FloodAI?


FloodAI was developed in the city of Montreal as part of a science fair project with the goal to develop better and more modern flood models in order to inform the population with accurate and real-time information about the threats that they face across the world.

A Science Fair Project

The project was created as part of a science fair project. Due to the cancellation of physical science fairs, an online science fair has been created by YSC. Click the button below to learn more about the project.

App Features

A website was built as part of the project. 

Real-time updates

The forecasts are synced in real time so that you can see which areas of the world are at risk of flooding. 

Customizable

The application allows you to see floods in the past. 

Global

FloodAI offers predictions at a global scale. 

Notifies you if you sign up

Our modern technology can allow you to be notified if your region is forecasted to be flooded. You can sign up with your email. Coming Soon.
